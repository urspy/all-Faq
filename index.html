SpyMC Rank System FAQ

Everything you need to know about Spy ranks, booster perks, and account linking

SPY Rank System

How do I get the SPY rank?

Use the command in our Discord:

!spy rank YourMinecraftName

This can only be done once per account.

Important

Your Minecraft name must match exactly, including capitalization.

Where can I use SPY rank commands?

All SPY rank commands must be used in the #🎀・ꜱᴘʏ-ʀᴀɴᴋ channel.

Important

Commands won't work in any other channels, even if you have the proper role.

What happens if I leave the Discord server?

If you leave the Discord server:

  • Your SPY rank will be automatically removed in-game
  • Your account link will remain in our system
  • If you rejoin, you'll need staff assistance to restore your rank

Booster Perks

How do I get booster perks?

Server boosters get special in-game perks. To activate them:

!booster YourMinecraftName

This must be done in #booster-channel (ID: 1362651428161585344).

Note

You must have the Booster role (ID: 1210582397750616084) to use this command.

What happens if I stop boosting?

When you stop boosting:

  • Your booster rank will be automatically downgraded to SPY rank
  • You'll keep your account link
  • If you boost again, you can reclaim your booster perks
Note

This happens automatically whether you remove the boost or leave the server.

Troubleshooting

My command isn't working

Common issues and solutions:

  • Wrong channel: Commands only work in designated channels
  • Missing role: Verify you have the required role
  • Typo in IGN: Your Minecraft name must match exactly
  • Already linked: Each account can only be linked once
  • Server offline: Check our status page for server issues
How do I contact support?

For assistance:

  • Open a ticket in our Discord
  • Email us at support@spymc.xyz
  • Message a staff member with the @Staff role

Please have your Discord ID and Minecraft IGN ready when contacting support.
